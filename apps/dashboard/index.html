The Process

From satellite to action
in under one second.

SAHARYN does not just collect data — it makes decisions. Here is exactly what happens from the moment a satellite captures a reading to the moment your operations team receives an alert.

01

NASA and ESA satellites record atmospheric conditions — AOD, wind, temperature, and dust plume signatures across your site.
02

Individual streams are fused using physics-based modeling to create a localized Dust Severity Index (DSI).
03
Industrial Telemetry Analysis
Causal models map environmental stressors against real-time mechanical vibration and performance telemetry.
04
Predictive Failure Detection
The system identifies risk patterns and generates precise operational recommendations for maintenance teams.
